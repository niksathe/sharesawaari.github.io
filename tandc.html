        You are only authorised to post a Ride if you fulfil all the following conditions:
            (i) You hold a valid driving licence.
            (ii) You only offer Rides for vehicles you own or use with the express permission of the owner, and in all cases that you are authorised to use for the purposes of carpooling.
            (iii) You are and remain the main driver of the vehicle subject of the Ride.
            (iv) The vehicle has valid third-party insurance.
            (v) You have no contraindication or medical incapacity for driving.
            (vi) The vehicle you intend to use for the Trip is a touring car with minimum 4 wheels.
            (vii) You do not intend posting another Ride for the same Trip on the Application.

            (ix) All Seats offered have a seatbelt, even if the vehicle is approved with seats having no seatbelt.

4.2.2.  Trip with Requested Booking
        When a Passenger is requested booking it will be accepted manually by the Driver. The booking request either accept or decline by the driver.
        The Passenger makes cash payment to the diver of the Cost Contribution when ride the completed.
